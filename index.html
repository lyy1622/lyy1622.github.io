Assessing the Design of AthenaHealth Electronic Medical Records

Interviews with workers at a local hospital were conducted to assess the strengths and weaknesses of the interface design for AthenaHealth Electronic Medical Records.

Redesigning the Williams College Study Abroad Website for International Students

We redesigned the Williams Global Education and Study Away website for a mobile phone format, with specific focus on tailoring the design for use by international students.

Gesturing to Play FireBoy, WaterGirl

By leveraging MediaPipe's advanced hand-tracking technology and refining our gestures through iterative testing, in this project we developed a functional prototype that allowed players to control Fireboy and Watergirl using only their hands.

Redesigning a Book Catalog Website to Improve Navigational Ability, Information Availability, and Visual Clarity

For this project, we improved the usability and navigational ability of a book catalog website by adding additional data about the books and expanding the options for filtering and sorting these titles.

Assessing the Accessibility of Online Shopping Websites for People with Motor Disabilities Using First-Person Accounts and Web Accessibility Standards

Drawing upon first-person accounts, surveys, web accessibility standards, and other academic writings on the subject, in this article I analyze the accessibility of online shopping, also known as E-commerce, websites for people with motor disabilities.

Design in Practice: Text-Summarizing Chrome Extension

We created an extension that allows the user to highlight text and select a preferred summarization option. The popup will provide them a clarified version of the original text which they can insert back into the original webpage.
